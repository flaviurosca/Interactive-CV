About

As an aspiring Front-End Web Developer, I am climbing the first steps towards a career in Web Development; fuelled by my enthusiastic and self-motivated nature to widen my knowledge, I've been learning more each day about web technologies.

Always willing to learn new skills and improve the current ones, I am used to working on different assignments that lead towards a goal. I am highly adaptable, so I can work well both in a team environment or by myself - remotely or in an office. Even though I lack experience right now, I bring lots of creativity on the table - after all, I've also been a musician since childhood. Oh, did I say that I'm a fast learner?

- Why Web Development?

My wife is a QA manager, so I am used to hearing about different mobile and web app issues and I am constantly fascinated about those behaviours; I also love solving puzzles, so this is why I wanted to understand more about "what's happening under the hood".

I started reading articles, which lead to online tutorials and courses like freeCodeCamp. At this point I realised that my curiousity has turned into passion so I took the next step: I enrolled on a Front-End Web Development course at The Informal School of IT (Școala Informală de IT) in order to develop my basic skills and advance towards a new career.
